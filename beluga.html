In the dark, the ocean swells.

An expansive oceanscape populated by parametric creatures, Beluga is alive and sentient, responsive to its environment and exists in perpetuity without ever repeating.

Over the course of 4 minutes, Beluga moves through the light cycle of the day, from dawn to dusk to the deep midnight blue of a moonless, starless night.

Beluga was originally created for the 120 foot long glass screen housed in the lobby of IAC Headquarters in NYC.

Beluga was performed at Big Screens 2011 with 2 dancers: Maddy Bullard and Lulu Soni.
